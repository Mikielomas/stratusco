Privacy Policy

Last Updated: February 2026

1. Introduction

Stratus Co ("we," "our," or "us"), a trading name of Arcus Cloud Computing Pty Ltd, is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard information when you interact with our services and content publishing tools.

2. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

  • Account Connection Data: When you connect social media accounts (such as Pinterest, Twitter, Facebook, Instagram, YouTube, LinkedIn, TikTok, or Threads), we store OAuth tokens to publish content on your behalf.
  • Usage Data: Basic analytics about how our tools are used, including content publishing metrics.
  • Contact Information: If you contact us directly, we may collect your email address and message content.

3. How We Use Your Information

We use collected information to:

  • Publish content to connected social media platforms on your behalf
  • Maintain and improve our publishing tools
  • Respond to your inquiries and support requests
  • Comply with legal obligations

4. Data Storage and Security

  • OAuth tokens and sensitive credentials are encrypted using AES-256 encryption
  • Data is stored securely and access is restricted
  • We do not sell, rent, or share your personal information with third parties for marketing purposes

5. Third-Party Services

Our tools integrate with third-party platforms including:

  • Pinterest
  • Twitter/X
  • Facebook and Instagram (Meta)
  • YouTube (Google)
  • LinkedIn
  • TikTok
  • Threads
  • Stripe (payment processing)
  • Amazon Associates

Each platform has its own privacy policy. We encourage you to review their policies. We only request the minimum permissions necessary to provide our services.

6. Affiliate Disclosure

Some content we publish may contain affiliate links. When you make a purchase through these links, we may earn a commission at no additional cost to you. We clearly disclose affiliate relationships in accordance with FTC guidelines.

7. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access the personal information we hold about you
  • Request correction of inaccurate information
  • Request deletion of your information
  • Disconnect your social media accounts at any time
  • Withdraw consent for data processing

8. Data Retention

We retain your information only as long as necessary to provide our services or as required by law. You may request deletion of your data at any time by contacting us.

9. Children's Privacy

Our services are not directed to children under 13. We do not knowingly collect information from children under 13.

10. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any changes by posting the new Privacy Policy on this page and updating the "Last Updated" date.
